SpaceWeave Anchor

Privacy Policy
Effective date: June 2025

✔  SpaceWeave Anchor does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Ever.

📡 What This App Does

SpaceWeave Anchor uses your device's camera and motion sensors to track your head position in 3D space using ARKit (iOS) or ARCore (Android). This position data is sent in real time over your local Wi-Fi network to SpaceWeave — an immersive projection system on the same network.

The app is a local network utility. It has no backend, no accounts, no analytics, and no internet connection.

🔍 Data We Access

  • Camera — used exclusively by ARKit/ARCore to compute your head position. Camera images are processed on-device and are never stored, transmitted, or seen by anyone.
  • Motion sensors (gyroscope, accelerometer) — used by ARKit/ARCore internally for tracking accuracy. Never stored or sent.
  • Local network — position coordinates (X, Y, Z in metres) are sent as UDP packets to an IP address you configure. This data goes only to your own SpaceWeave system on your local network.
  • App settings — your chosen IP address, port number, and send rate are saved locally on your device using standard device storage (SharedPreferences / UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

🚫 Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location (GPS) data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No usage analytics or crash reports sent to any server
  • No camera images or video footage
  • No biometric data of any kind
  • No data is uploaded to any cloud service or third party

🤝 Third-Party Services

SpaceWeave Anchor does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection.

The following platform-provided frameworks are used:

  • ARKit (Apple) — on-device AR tracking on iOS. Apple's own privacy policy governs ARKit.
  • ARCore (Google) — on-device AR tracking on Android. Google's own privacy policy governs ARCore.

Neither ARKit nor ARCore transmit camera or sensor data off your device in the context of this app.

🔐 Permissions Explained

  • Camera (required) — ARKit and ARCore need the camera to compute your position in space. Without camera access the app cannot track position.
  • Local Network (iOS) — required to send position data over UDP to your SpaceWeave system on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • Internet (Android) — the INTERNET permission is used solely for local UDP communication on your home/venue network. No internet traffic is generated.

👶 Children's Privacy

SpaceWeave Anchor does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is a local network tool with no data collection of any kind.

🗑️ Data Retention & Deletion

The only data stored by the app is your connection settings (IP address, port, send rate) saved locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app. No data exists on any server to delete.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the new version will be published at the same URL with an updated effective date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
